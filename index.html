Visit Cocoa Beach, Florida

A family-friendly destination located on Florida's Space Coast, just 45 minutes of Orlando.

Top three activities to do in Cocoa Beach

Learn how to surf

Cocoa Beach is the place to learn how to surf. Either if you're a kid or adult, you'll be able to enjoy the Atlantic waves.

Watch a rocket launch

Watch from the beach, from the river or from the Pier a live rocket launch and experience the feeling of the space industry.

Visit Kennedy Space Center

Come and visit the Kennedy Space Center and have the opportunity to feel like an astronout for a day. Learn everything about NASA and Space.
